Misc


From pedal graphics to T-shirts to book covers, I try to constrain my designs by using free and open source tools such as Inkscape and GIMP. I use Arch, by the way.

In addition to graphic design, I am very experienced in more traditional art forms. Many of my guitar pedals are hand-painted or hand-engraved.
